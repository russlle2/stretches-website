About Joshua Miller

Known to the streets and the industry as GMF Productions, Joshua Miller is a rising force in the rap scene. Representing the authentic sound of the Southside, his music delivers raw lyricism, undeniable grit, and heavy beats.

Under his own imprint, GETTIN' MONEY FOREVER Productions LLC, Joshua released his highly acclaimed track "Aint No Sunshine" in early 2026, solidifying his spot. Between high-energy records like "Get Em' Gone" and "Big Smoke", and a rapidly growing streetwear line, GMF Productions is building an empire.
