Shipping Policy

We currently ship domestically within the United States. Orders over $100 ship free via standard ground service. Orders are processed within 2–5 business days, excluding weekends and holidays. Once your order ships you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information.

International shipping is not available on this preview site. All shipping times are estimates and may vary by destination and carrier demand.
